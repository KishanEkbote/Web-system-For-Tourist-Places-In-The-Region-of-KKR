Visit The Places At Bidar

Bidar Famous PLaces

Bidar, situated in the heart of the Kalyana Karnataka region, boasts a plethora of famous landmarks that embody the area’s rich historical and cultural heritage. These attractions serve as significant symbols of Bidar’s importance within Kalyana Karnataka, drawing visitors from far and wide to explore their unique charm and historical significance.

Bidar Fort

Bidar Fort, a majestic architectural marvel located in the heart of Bidar city, stands as a testament to the region’s rich history and cultural heritage. Built during the Bahmani Sultanate era in the 15th century, this imposing fortification served as a symbol of power and authority, guarding the strategic approaches to the city.

Bidar Tomb

Raichur Fort, an ancient stronghold located in Raichur, Karnataka, stands as a testament to the region’s rich historical heritage. Built during the Kakatiya dynasty, this formidable fortification offers visitors a glimpse into the past with its architectural marvels and panoramic vistas of the surrounding landscape

Basavakalya

Basavakalyan holds special significance in the history of Lingayatism, a distinct Shaivite sect founded by the 12th-century philosopher and social reformer, Basavanna. The town is home to the revered Anubhava Mantapa, where Basavanna established a platform for spiritual discourse and social reform, promoting ideals of equality, devotion, and social justice.
